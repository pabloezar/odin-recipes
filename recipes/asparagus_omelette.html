Asparagus Omelette

Description

This light and flavorful asparagus omelette is a perfect quick meal for breakfast, brunch, or a light lunch. It combines fluffy eggs with tender-crisp asparagus, offering a nutritious and delicious start to your day. Customize it with your favorite cheese or herbs for an even more satisfying experience!

It's incredibly simple to make, requiring just a few common ingredients and minimal cooking time, making it ideal for busy mornings.

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp milk or water
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp olive oil or butter
  • 6-8 asparagus spears, tough ends snapped off and cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 2 tbsp shredded cheese (cheddar, Gruyère, or feta work well) - optional
  • Fresh chives or parsley, chopped, for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare Asparagus: Heat olive oil or butter in a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Add the asparagus pieces and sauté for 3-5 minutes, or until tender-crisp. Remove asparagus from the skillet and set aside.
  2. Whisk Eggs: In a medium bowl, whisk the eggs with milk or water, salt, and pepper until light and frothy.
  3. Cook Omelette: Return the skillet to medium-low heat. If needed, add a tiny bit more oil or butter. Pour the egg mixture into the hot skillet. Let it cook undisturbed for about 1-2 minutes until the edges begin to set.
  4. Add Fillings: Once the top is mostly set but still a little runny, sprinkle the cooked asparagus over one half of the omelette. If using, sprinkle with cheese.
  5. Fold and Serve: Carefully fold the other half of the omelette over the filling. Cook for another 30 seconds to 1 minute, or until the cheese is melted and the omelette is cooked to your desired doneness. Slide onto a plate and garnish with fresh chives or parsley, if desired. Serve immediately.
